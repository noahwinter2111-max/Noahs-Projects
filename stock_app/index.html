How It Works

This is a paper trading simulator — you use virtual money to practice buying and selling real stocks. Nothing here costs real money.

  • Watchlist (left side): 20 popular stocks with live prices. Green = price is up today, Red = price is down today. Click any stock to see details.
  • Charts: See how the price has moved over time. Green line = price went up in that period. Red line = price went down. Use the time buttons (1W, 1M, 1Y, etc.) to zoom in or out.
  • Buying: Pick how many shares you want, then click Buy. The cost comes out of your virtual cash balance.
  • Selling: When you own a stock, a Sell button appears. Selling gives you back cash at the current price. If the price went up since you bought, you profit. If it went down, you lose money.
  • Portfolio: Click the Portfolio tab to see everything you own, your total value, and whether you're up or down overall.
  • Stats: Every number below the chart has an explanation. These are the same stats real investors use to evaluate stocks.

Your trades are saved in your browser. Refresh or come back later and everything is still there.

This is a simulator for educational purposes only. Stock prices are real but delayed. This is not financial advice. Do not make real investment decisions based on this app.
